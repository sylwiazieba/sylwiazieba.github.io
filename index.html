Exciteam

App Concept, Design & Prototypes

Committed to creating a focused and positive work enviornment: welcome Exciteam. Based on organizational psychology and management principles, I drew out wireframes and created UX prototypes in Adobe XD and Principle to make this idea come to life. Exciteam provides motivational quotes, creates detailed schedules, and allows co-workers to plan weekends and volunteer opportunities together.

iDriveYourCar

Website Development, Design & Copy Writing

A company that strives to provide the highest-quality chauffers to its clients deserves a flawless website and mobile app. I built an internal page for the development team which allowed us to implement our ideas into reality. Following PSD designs, we created a new & clean brand for iDriveYourCar using HTML, CSS, AngularJS, and MeteorJS. We also developed driver and client registration and reservation forms for the Operations team. Using Ionic, the mobile app for the driver and client was started utilizing push notifications and token-based authentication.
