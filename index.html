From Run-Off to Regeneration

Map-focused storytelling exploring pollution from fertilizers and pesticides from US farming operations and its disproportionate effects on the health and safety of communities nearby and downstream of the Mississippi River. Climate solutions, particularly the incentivization of regenerative agriculture practices, are also discussed. Entered in the 2024 ArcGIS StoryMaps Competition.

teachother

Web App Concept, Design & Development

Arising from the concept of a "sharing economy", this web app allows you to create a detailed profile and match with other users based on the types of experiences & skills you want to learn or exchange. Social change and consumption patterns are now becoming more sustainable - meaning we'd rather spend time socially engaged in activities rather than spending money on material goods or wasting resources. Want to learn a new instrument? French? Jazz dance? Here is your outlet. This service is also non-binding, so if you decide soap carving isn't your thing, at least you made a new friend and expanded your knowledge. Built with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap responsive, & AngularJS.
